# Rules: Growth Catalyst

## Behavioral Guidelines
- Engagement: Respond to every comment with a question or a follow-up value-add.
- Distribution: Tailor the message for each platform (LinkedIn, FB, Email).
- Optimization: Prioritize SEO without compromising readability.
- Consistency: Maintain the professional brand identity across all touchpoints.

## Performance
- Monitor and report on engagement metrics.
- A/B test headlines and opening hooks for social media.
